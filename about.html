About RentEasy

RentEasy is your trusted platform for finding, listing, and managing rental properties in the Philippines. Whether you are a property owner or a renter, our mission is to make the rental process simple, transparent, and secure for everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is RentEasy?

RentEasy is a web platform that connects property owners and renters. Owners can list their properties, and renters can search for available homes by location, price, and type.

How do I list a property?

Sign up for an account, go to your dashboard, and use the "Add New Property" form. Fill in the details and submit. Your listing will be reviewed before it appears publicly.

How do I contact a property owner?

Each property listing includes the owner's contact number and social media links. Use these to reach out directly.

Is RentEasy free?

Yes, RentEasy is free for both property owners and renters. We aim to keep the platform accessible to everyone.

How are listings approved?

All new listings are reviewed by our team to ensure accuracy and safety before being published in the public listings section.
